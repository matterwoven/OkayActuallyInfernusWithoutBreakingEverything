# Introduction
Adds a new survivor: Infernus.

## Skills
Passive: Afterburn | Incendiary Remarks applies stacks of buildup on hit, afterburn deals 200% damage per second. Intensifies every ten stacks.\n
Primary: Incenidary Remarks | Shoot flaming bullets dealing 60% damage.\n
Secondary: Napalm | Eject napalm to coat enemies dealing 250% damage. The Napalm status effect increases damage taken by 30%.\n
Utility: Flame Dash | Dash swiftly leaving behind a trail of flames, enemies standing on it take 200% damage over time.\n
Special: Concussive Combustion | Turn into a living bomb, dealing 800% damage to nearby enemies.\n
